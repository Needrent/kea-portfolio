The Project

We had to choose a style from what the teachers had selected and I chose the style "Year Walk" from the game of the same name.

What did I learn?

I learned that I still got a lot to learn, but also that I can do more that I give my self credit for.

The most challenging?

Getting the textures to work was harder than expected because Illutrator handles that type of files poorly, compaired to Photoshop or After Effect.

In the end I event had to scrap them since I, had been sick that week and lost a lot of time, so had to focus on something simple in the animation area.
